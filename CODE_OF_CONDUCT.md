# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Give constructive feedback
- Focus on what's best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive comments
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information
- Any other unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.
